About Me

Hi! I am Xinlei Yu, an incoming PhD student of CUHK@MMLab, supervised by Prof. Xiangyu Yue. Previously, I work as research assistant in NUS@LV-Lab, supervised by Prof. Shuicheng Yan and Dr. Xiaobin Hu. I have published several top conferences and journals, such as CVPR, ICLR, ICML, ACM MM, etc.

My research has a broad focus, covering multimodal AI, agentic AI, and embodied AI. Please feel free to contact me for any discussion or collaboration.
